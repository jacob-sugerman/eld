About Ecology Landscape Design — Ecological Landscaping in the Twin Cities

𓆏 about
Your yard is an ecosystem — part of a larger whole, or bioregion, where you live. By responding to the particular ecological roles and needs of your land, we create a healthy and welcoming habitat for the plants, wildlife, and humans it supports. Our approach works with rather than against the native plant communities, soil composition, and water in your area. With expertise in design, ecology, installation, and maintenance, we'll help you bring your landscape into balance with nature.

Ecological Landscaping Services in Saint Paul & Twin Cities

We integrate the disciplines of plant science, water management, and ecological stewardship to create landscapes that inspire and endure. Our designs are more than just plans: they are living systems, meticulously rendered through comprehensive master plans, site-specific drawings, and detailed documentation. Each design is a collaborative journey, enriched by full-scale color presentations and tailored ecological insights. Often (but not always) produced alongside consultations, designs include full-size color prints of a master design, site map, and detailed documentation. Our design process supports your project from concept to completion.

Our ecological consultation provides a deeper understanding of your land's natural assets. We assess your landscape using tools like GIS mapping, civil datasets, and soil testing, combined with on-site evaluations. This allows us to design a management plan that addresses current challenges, such as erosion, drainage issues, or invasive species, and fosters long-term growth and sustainability. Our tailored recommendations include plant species selection, water conservation techniques, and habitat restoration strategies, ensuring your landscape remains vibrant and healthy.

Our installation process blends craftsmanship with ecological support. We prioritize sustainable techniques, using hand-built methods when possible that minimize disruption. Whether installing plants or building custom features like stone pathways and planters, we approach every detail with intention. The result is a thoughtfully crafted space that grows in harmony with its surroundings.

Design

We integrate the disciplines of plant science, water management, and ecological stewardship to create landscapes that inspire and endure. Our designs are more than just plans—they are living systems, meticulously rendered through comprehensive master plans, site-specific drawings, and detailed documentation. Each design is a collaborative journey, enriched by full-scale color presentations and tailored ecological insights. Often (but not always) produced alongside consultations, designs include full-size color prints of a master design, site map, and detailed documentation. Our design process supports your project from concept to completion.

Consultation

Our ecological consultation provides a deeper understanding of your land’s natural assets. We assess your landscape using tools like GIS mapping, civil datasets, and soil testing, combined with on-site evaluations. This allows us to design a management plan that addresses current challenges—such as erosion, drainage issues, or invasive species—and fosters long-term growth and sustainability. Our tailored recommendations include plant species selection, water conservation techniques, and habitat restoration strategies, ensuring your landscape remains vibrant and healthy. We partner with you and the land to restore ecological balance, enhance natural beauty, and create resilient systems that stand the test of time.

Installation

Our installation process blends craftsmanship with ecological support. We prioritize sustainable techniques, using low-disturbance, hand-built methods when possible to protect soils, existing tree roots, and the integrity of your site as we work. We only use regional ecotype native plants sourced through local growers— ensuring that plantings are truly adapted to our climate and wildlife. Avoiding pesticides entirely, we rely on careful site prep, thoughtful mulch application, and durable construction methods that help plantings establish cleanly. Whether installing native plant communities, grading for stormwater management, or building custom dry stone features like walls and pathways, we approach every detail with intention. The result is a thoughtfully crafted space that settles in beautifully and grows in harmony with its surroundings.

Maintenance

Ecological landscapes are dynamic living systems. After installation, the quieter work of monitoring, adaptive management, and seasonal adjustment often determines whether plantings truly thrive. Because native plant communities are adapted to our local climate and soils, they typically require less care as they become more established. Our approach to maintenance is practical and flexible, built around timely stewardship visits that keep your landscape healthy, attractive, and functioning as intended. We focus on the right work at the right moment—targeted weeding and invasive control, selective thinning, and occasional design modifications as the site matures. We also keep an eye on drainage patterns, soil moisture, and plant performance over time so systems stays resilient through wet seasons, drought, and everything in between.

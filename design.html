Consultation

Our ecological consultation provides a deeper understanding of a landscapes’s natural assets. We conduct an on-site assessment of your landscape and using tools like soil testing, GIS mapping, and drainage analysis. This allows us to develop a management plan that addresses current challenges—such as erosion, drainage issues, or invasive species—and fosters long-term growth and sustainability. Our tailored recommendations include plant species inventories, water conservation techniques, and habitat restoration strategies, ensuring your landscape remains vibrant and healthy. We partner with you and the land to restore ecological balance, enhance natural beauty, and create resilient systems that stand the test of time.

Design

We draw from the disciplines of permaculture, land art, and ecology to create landscape designs that heal and endure. Our designs go beyond the technical, aiming to emulate the living systems they represent through comprehensive plans, full-size color prints, hand drawings, computer renderings, and illustrated documentation. Each design is a collaborative journey, enriched by in-deapth presentations and tailored ecological insights. Our design process supports your project from concept through realization.

Installation

Our installation process blends craftsmanship with ecological support. We prioritize sustainable techniques, using low-disturbance, hand-built methods when possible to protect soils, existing tree roots, and the integrity of your site as we work. We only use regional ecotype native plants sourced through local growers—ensuring that plantings are truly adapted to our climate and wildlife. Avoiding harmful pesticides, we rely on careful site prep, thoughtful mulch application, and durable construction methods that help plantings establish cleanly. Whether installing native plant communities, replacing non-native lawns with ecological alternatives, grading for stormwater management, or building custom dry stone features like walls, patios, and pathways, we approach every detail with intention. The result is a thoughtfully crafted space that settles in beautifully and grows in harmony with its surroundings.

Maintenance

After installation, the quieter work of monitoring, adaptive management, and ongoing stewardship often determines whether plantings truly thrive. Because native plant communities are adapted to our local climate and soils, they typically require less care as they become more established. Our approach to ecological maintenance is practical and flexible, built around timely interventions that keep your landscape healthy, beautiful, and functioning as intended. We focus on the right work at the right moment—targeted management of water, weeding, pruning, organic material, and occasional modifications as the site matures.
